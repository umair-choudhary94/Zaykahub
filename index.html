Our Story

The passion behind ZaykaHub

A Tradition of Excellence

Founded in 2010, ZaykaHub has been serving the community with passion and dedication for over a decade. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a place where people could enjoy exceptional food in a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Our chef, Michael Roberts, brings over 20 years of culinary expertise from around the world. Each dish is crafted with care using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients.
